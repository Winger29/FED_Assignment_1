    • Porsche 919 Evo

    The Porsche 919 Evo is a legendary race car known for its groundbreaking performance and engineering. An evolution of the 919 Hybrid, the 919 Evo was developed after Porsche's successful run in the FIA World Endurance Championship and showcased the pinnacle of Porsche’s hybrid technology. With a combined output of around 1,160 horsepower from its 2.0-liter V4 turbo engine and energy recovery system, the 919 Evo set unprecedented records and redefined motorsport standards.

    Notable achievements include the record-setting 5:19.546 lap at the Nürburgring Nordschleife—making it the fastest car ever on the circuit—and a 3:16.803 time at the Pikes Peak International Hill Climb, setting a new benchmark for hill climb racing. With its lightweight construction, advanced aerodynamics, and extraordinary power, the 919 Evo remains an icon of automotive excellence and a testament to Porsche's engineering prowess.

    Specifications

    • Engine: 2.0L V4 Hybrid
    • Power: 434 hp (electric) + 711 hp (gasoline)
    • Top Speed: 370 km/h
    • Acceleration (0-100 km/h): 2.2 seconds
    • Weight: 849 kg
